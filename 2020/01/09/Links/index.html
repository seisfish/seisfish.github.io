Open Access Geo-databases

Posted on 2020-01-09 Edited on 2020-01-10 In Resource , Data

It is wise for researchers to take advantage of open data. Sometimes it can be crucial. Some of the greatest open access geo-databases are as follows.

Seafloor Bathymetry

Global high resolution

GMRT

JAMSTEC - Data and Sample Research System for Whole Cruise Information - Remember to choose “Bathymetry (MBES)” before searching!

NOAA Bathymetry

Regional high resolution

IHO Data Centre for Digital Bathymetry

NOAA - Altimetric Bathymetry

Baltic Sea Bathymetry Database

AusSeabed Bathymetry and Backscatter Data, Australia

General Bathymetric Chart of the Oceans - 15 arc seconds

GEBCO 2019 Gridded Bathymetry Data

GEBCO - Undersea Feature Names Gazetteer

Seismology

Earthquake Catalog

Bulletin of the ISC

IRIS Event Plot Product Query

USGS Earthquake Catalog

AusPass, Australia

Data Centers Supporting FDSN Web Services

ISC Station Search

FDSN Network Codes

Focal Mechanism

Global CMT

ISC Bulletin - Focal mechanism

NIED F-net Earthquake Mechanism Information

Istituto Nazionale Di Geofisica E Vulcanologia - Time Domain Moment Tensor Catalogue, Italy

BATS CMT Catalog Search, Taiwan

Earth Model

CRUST 1.0: Global Crustal Model at 1x1 Degrees

IRIS Earth Model Collaboration

ICGEM - Gravity Field Models

Gravity

Marine Gravity Map

Geomagnetism

Geomagnetic and Electric Field Models

Heat Flow

Global Heat Flow Database

Volcano

Volcano Location Database Search
